AirPods Max

The AirPods Max are Apple’s premium over-ear headphones, designed for high-fidelity audio and seamless integration with the Apple ecosystem. They feature Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and Transparency Mode, allowing users to switch between immersive sound and environmental awareness. The headphones are equipped with Apple's custom acoustic design, including a 40mm dynamic driver for rich bass and clear highs. They utilize the H1 chip in each ear cup for computational audio, enabling features like Adaptive EQ and Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking. The AirPods Max offer a battery life of up to 20 hours with ANC and Spatial Audio active, and they charge via a Lightning to USB-C cable.
