AirPods 4

The AirPods 4 offer significant improvements over previous models, including features like Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and enhanced Adaptive Audio technology. These earbuds are designed to work seamlessly with iOS 18 or later and are compatible across Apple's ecosystem. They boast IP54-rated dust, sweat, and water resistance, making them suitable for workouts and light outdoor use. With a charging case that supports both USB-C and wireless charging, the AirPods 4 provide up to 6 hours of listening time on a single charge (with ANC enabled) and 30 hours with the case. The addition of Spatial Audio and personalized sound profiles enhances the listening experience, particularly when paired with devices equipped with a TrueDepth camera for precise head-tracking capabilities.
